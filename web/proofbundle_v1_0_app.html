ProofBundle v1.0.0

A sealed, verifiable claim object. Five top-level fields: hdr, payload, meta, refs, seal.

Sealing computes canonical bytes (PB-CANON-JSON-1), digests them, signs the digest. Verification recomputes and checks.

Algorithms

Digests: SHA-256, SHA-384, SHA-512, BLAKE3, BLAKE2b. Signatures: Ed25519, ECDSA-P256/384/521, RSA-PSS-2048/3072/4096.

Eleven outcomes

verified, malformed, invalid-signature, out-of-bounds, unknown-version, missing-side-info, lineage-invalid, resource-exhausted, policy-denied, indeterminate, not-defined-in-this-version.

Profiles

PB-INTEGRITY-1 (signature only), PB-BOUNDARY-1 (+ boundary), PB-LINEAGE-1 (+ parents), PB-REGULATED-1 (+ HITL).
